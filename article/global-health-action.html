Global Health Action

Global Health Action publishes studies in the global health field narrow health information gaps and contribute to the implementation of policies and actions that lead to improved global health. The journal is indexed in Scopus, Web of Science, and PubMed.

Articles selected for publication in this journal are currently being edited.
